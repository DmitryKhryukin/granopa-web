Research document for a psychologist's personal website — structure, SEO, legal (152-ФЗ), platform choice, and content strategy.

## What it does

Collects research on building a personal website for a psychologist working online from Saint Petersburg. Covers page structure, contact strategy (no phone — messengers only), Yandex SEO, blog platforms (Telegram + site), 152-ФЗ compliance, and Tilda as the default platform.

## Key decisions

- Tilda Personal as default platform (unless there's a clear reason for custom)
- No phone number on the site — form + Telegram/WhatsApp only
- Yandex-first SEO strategy with medium/low-frequency queries
